Interesting Facts


1. Sand Cats Share Their Name With an Alcoholic Drink

This small feline goes by the names "sand cat" and "sand dune cat," but its scientific name is a bit more interesting: Felis margarita.


2. They Are the Only Cat That Lives Primarily in the Desert

Sand cats do not need much water at all. They can go for weeks without a single sip, getting all the moisture they need from the prey they consume.


3. Sand Cats Bark Like Dogs

When taking a break from its solitary lifestyle and looking for a mate, the sand cat uses mews and bark-like vocalizations as a mating call. The sounds have been likened to the high-pitched rasps of small dogs like chihuahuas.
